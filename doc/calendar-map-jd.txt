data/calendar/map-jd-*.txt
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

These files are date mapping files
<https://manakai.github.io/spec-datetime/#date-mapping-file> from all
the Julian Day Number (JDN) integers in the range [1000000, 2525000]
to dates in calendars.

  data/calendar/map-jd-gregorian.txt

    Mapping to the Gregorian date strings
    <https://manakai.github.io/spec-datetime/#gregorian-date-string>.

  data/calendar/map-jd-julian.txt

    Mapping to the Julian date strings
    <https://manakai.github.io/spec-datetime/#julian-date-string>.
